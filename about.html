About Us

We are focused on delivering custom pre-engineered building (PEB) solutions, offering end-to-end services in design, fabrication, and installation. Our structures ensure faster construction, reduced costs, and customizable designs to meet specific project needs. Committed to innovation, safety, and environmental protection, we deliver reliable, economical steel structures with the highest professionalism..

Genesis of Tech Meeco Infrastructure Private Limited:

We are Meeco Engineering, a well-established company specializing in all aspects of steel structural fabrication and manufacturing, with a strong presence in the Eastern region. Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence, partnering with esteemed organizations such as L&T, GE Power India Ltd, ISGEC, Thermax Group, Paharpur Cooling Tower and GEA Process Engineering for PSU and high-profile private projects. Operating four units in the Durgapur area, we are proud to maintain a production capacity of 500 to 600 metric tons of finished materials per month.

In addition to our proven track record in manufacturing and fabrication, we are excited to introduce our new venture Tech Meeco Infra Private Limited. This division will focus on providing comprehensive solutions for Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB). Our offerings will include prefab components such as C/Z purlins, downcomers, eave gutters, ridge vents and custom-designed flashing items to meet the client's specific project needs.

At Tech Meeco Infra Pvt Ltd, we have amassed a team of highly skilled experts and engineers dedicated to delivering high-quality services while ensuring timely delivery. We employ the latest industry technologies to provide innovative and cost-effective solutions, tailored to meet the unique requirements of our clients.
